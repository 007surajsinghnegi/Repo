About Us

India ranks among the best in the world in terms of medical value travel across the globe. India has best hospitals, renowned doctors, state-of-the-art infrastructure, traditional hospitality and the best economical surgery packages. According to the Confederation of Indian Industries (CII), the primary reason that attracts medical value travel to India is cost-effectiveness and treatment from accredited facilities at par with developed countries at much lower cost.

India Treatment Help is dedicated to improve & empower health of people and changing lives of patient by providing them quality healthcare services at cost effective prices. We assist patient to find diagnostic & therapeutic treatment in India.
The company is associated with intellectual & renowned doctors across the country to assist patient in choosing appropriate treatment at cost effective prices. Our focus is on technological advance treatment with value added services to patients.

India Treatment Help is key to affordable and accessible healthcare services as most of people have to bear healthcare expenses as ‘’ out of pocket’’ expenses. With experience of more than 10 years in serving and helping patients
we are working on beginning to end help model for providing help, guidance & support in finding correct diagnosis and right treatment plan for the patient with no extra cost. We are associated with multiple hospitals for over a decade now and have been serving patients by working as a bridge between the patient and healthcare provider.

Selection of right doctor and right hospital is also an important decision which patient
decides before initiating treatment. We help patient in selecting the right doctor for their treatment by discussing the reports with experience and renowned doctors and hospital.

Since patients have multiple queries in beginning of the treatment and hospitals are not able to provide personal touch to every query. We bridge patient and doctor to give valuable time to resolve medical queries of the patients who are not in India as we coordinate with the hospitals directly on patient behalf with no extra cost.
